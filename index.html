📍 Malta Travel Guide

Malta is one of the best destinations in Europe to explore by car. Renting a vehicle gives you full freedom to discover hidden beaches, historical towns, and scenic coastal roads.

Public transport can be slow during summer, so having your own car saves time and gives flexibility.

🚦 Driving Tips in Malta

  • Drive on the left side
  • Roads are narrow in old towns
  • Watch for scooters and pedestrians
  • Parking is limited in busy areas
  • Small cars are easier to drive
